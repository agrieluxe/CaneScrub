Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cane Scrub?

A sugarcane bagasse loofah is an eco-friendly exfoliating product made from the fibrous residue of sugarcane after juice extraction. It is biodegradable and serves as a natural alternative to synthetic sponges and loofahs.

How do I use a sugarcane bagasse loofah?

To use a sugarcane bagasse loofah, wet it with water and apply your favorite soap or body wash. Gently scrub your skin in circular motions to exfoliate and cleanse. Rinse the loofah after use and hang it to dry.

Is a sugarcane bagasse loofah suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, a sugarcane bagasse loofah is gentle and effective for sensitive skin. Its natural fibers provide exfoliation without causing irritation, making it a great choice for individuals with delicate skin.

How long does a sugarcane bagasse loofah last?

With proper care, a sugarcane bagasse loofah can last several weeks to a few months. To extend its lifespan, rinse it thoroughly after each use, allow it to dry completely, and store it in a dry place.

Is a sugarcane bagasse loofah environmentally friendly?

Absolutely! Sugarcane bagasse loofahs are made from a renewable resource and are fully biodegradable. By choosing this product, you are supporting sustainable practices and reducing plastic waste associated with synthetic sponges.

How do I clean and care for my Cane Scrub?

To clean your sugarcane bagasse loofah, rinse it thoroughly after each use and allow it to dry completely to prevent mold and bacteria growth. You can occasionally soak it in a mixture of warm water and vinegar to disinfect it. Replace the loofah every few weeks, depending on usage, to ensure optimal hygiene.
